Key Design Decisions

Tap, Don't Type

Everything is sliders, toggles, or tap selections. Only notes field needs typing.

Side Effect Sliders

0-5 severity scale for each symptom. Quick to adjust, shows at-a-glance severity.

Steppers for Diet

+/- buttons for meals, protein (10g steps), water (0.5L steps). Fast and precise.

Activity Sliders

Steps (0-20k) and duration (0-120min) as sliders. Toggle for workout type.

No Injection on Log Page

Injection logging stays on the Jabs page. The Log page only shows injection data when viewing past days (read-only display).
